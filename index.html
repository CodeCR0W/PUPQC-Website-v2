PUP Quezon City

The PUP Quezon City Campus emerged from community petitions and negotiations with local officials to establish a higher education institution for youth in the National Government Center area. Since its inception on March 24, 1997, the campus has been dedicated to providing quality education and affordable tuition, prioritizing the urban poor communities of Quezon City, particularly those from the National Government Center Housing Project (NGCHP). It stands as a testament to the successful efforts in the ongoing fight against ignorance.

PUP Mission

Ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education and promoting lifelong learning opportunities through a re-engineered polytechnic university by committing to:
● Provide democratized access to educational opportunities for the holistic development of individuals with global perspective
● Offer industry-oriented curricula that produce highly-skilled professionals with managerial and technical capabilities and a strong sense of public service for nation building
● Embed a culture of research and innovation continuously develop faculty and employees with the highest level of professionalism
● Engage public and private institutions and other stakeholders for the attainment of social development goalestablish a strong presence and impact in the international academic community

The PUP Philosophy

As a state university, the Polytechnic University of the Philippines believes that:
● Education is an instrument for the development of the citizenry and for the enhancement of nation building; and
● That meaningful growth and transformation of the country are best achieved in an atmosphere of brotherhood, peace, freedom, justice and nationalist-oriented education imbued with the spirit of humanist internationalism.
